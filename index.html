What is Rapid Hacks?

Rapid Hacks is a 24-hour online hackathon with tracks designed specifically for beginners. No matter if you are still ideating your solution, designing it or have a prototype ready ~ Everyone is invited!

Resources

We will be providing beginner freindly resources for each and every domain.

Setup Support

If you are facing any issues in setting up your system environment, don't worry! we will help you out

Presentation Sessions

We will be taking sessions on each and every domain which will make the use of resources easier!

Review Sessions

We will be providing review sessions to anyone who needs help.

Mentor Support

Apart from the sessions and resources each team will have their own mentor who will be available to help them out 24*7.

Expert Sessions

We will be inviting guest speakers from various domains to share their experience in their areas of expertise.

TRACKS & THEME


The hackathon has tracks designed specially for beginners with no-code, low-code and coding tracks:

  • IDEATE TRACK: The hackers can submit their idea.
  • DESIGN TRACK: The hackers have to present a design of what their solution would look like.
  • SOLVE TRACK: The hackers have to present a solution to the problem statement that they have chosen.

The theme for Rapid Hacks is building Sustainable Technology. However, the hackers can also submit projects based on any other theme under the Open Innovation Category.

TIMELINE

The hacking period is of 24-hours starting from 12:00 Noon IST, 18th February 2023 to 12:00 Noon IST, 19th February 2023. Lots of fun events, mini events and speaker sessions will take place during the course of the hackathon in which all the hackers can participate.

  • 10:00 AM: Opening Ceremony
  • 12:00 Noon: Hacking Period Begins
  • 6:00 PM: Mini Event 1
  • 10:00 PM: Fun Session
  • 12:00 Noon: Hacking Period Ends
  • 2:00 PM: Mini Event 2
  • 4:00 PM: Guest Speaker Session
  • 7:00 PM: Closing Ceremony

The results for all the events will be announced during the closing ceremony.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is the hackathon in online mode?

    Yes, Rapid Hacks is a 24-hour online hackathon challenge.

  • What is the minimum/maximum team size?

    Each team can contain 1-4 hackers.

  • Where can we connect with the mentors?

    You can connect with our mentors and interact with the other hackers on our discord server.

  • Can we submit old projects?

    Yes, you can submit the projects made before the hacking period.

  • Can we take part in more than one track?

    Yes, you can take part in all the three tracks at once.
